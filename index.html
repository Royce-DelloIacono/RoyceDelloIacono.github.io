DEDICATION

- - - THE FOLLOWING IS A DEDICATION FROM UNCLE SHAWN - - -



Sherry loved Royce with every cell in her body. Royce got to experience a beautiful gift having Sherry for a mom.

She gave him everything she had to give, and everything she could.


--- This photo says to me ---

Sherry is happy and having fun with her son. Sherry has an absolutely gorgeous smile.

Royce is a cutie. Instead of smiling for the camera, he snarls. What a beautiful child?


I am the PROUD Uncle of Royce Louis Dello Iacono, second born of all my nieces and nephews. I love my deceased nephew and will never forget how beautiful a spirit he presented in the physical world. Royce is a powerful soul that was ready to change the world with his positive energy and insight. He was always authentic when he met new people; which drew them in, because he didn't have a guard up like most people do in this life. It hurts me that he is not here anymore. But, I know where he went, and I am so happy for him.

His whole life he wanted a father, and now, he is with, THE FATHER.

A Fond Memory #1

  • Uncle Shawn
  • 03 March 2023

I remember one time Sherry and Royce came to visit me in Mobile, Alabama. I was expecting them, and was looking forward to spending time with Royce. So, I went out and bought Royce a BB Gun. I was going to teach him how to shoot. He was 5 or 6 years old, at the time. He was so excited to receive the BB gun, and we took time to shoot at targets in the back yard. I wanted Royce to take the BB gun home, to continue practicing. Sherry said, "No way! He'll shoot me with it". I think that's so cute.

A Fond Memory #2

  • Uncle Shawn
  • 25 March 2023

One time I was baby sitting Royce at home. He wanted to know how strong I was and asked me to squeeze his hand as hard as I could. So, I obliged. He was screaming, "Owwww!", and laughing at the same time. As soon as I would let go he would ask me to do it again. When it was his turn to squeeze my hand he would use both hands and I would act as if nothing was happening. He couldn't believe I didn't feel anything when he tried.

A Fond Memory #3

  • Uncle Shawn
  • 27 March 2023

One time, when Royce came to visit, I decided to take him to work to introduce my nephew to my co-workers. I worked on a campus and there was a festival taking place. Food was being given out, and activities were available. Royce and I had fun and were walking home. He spotted a popcorn ball that someone had dropped, and quickly ran over to picked it up. He immediately ate it, without hesitation. I was disgusted, but he loved it. He acted as if he discovered a treasure, and never even considered it germy. Just a typical child, no concerns.

A Fond Memory #4

  • Shawn W. Feltmate (Aka: Uncle Shawn)
  • 23 June 2023

I remember one time I was visiting Sherry in Citra, Florida (she lived in an old country store that was converted into an apartment). Royce was doing school work on a laptop in the kitchen. Sherry wasn't sure if he was actually doing his school work. So she asked him, and he told her that he was studying. To be sure he wasn't lying (because young adolescence lie for some reason, at a certain age/time of their lives). Sherry then goes outside the apartment and peeked in a window that Royce's back was facing. GUESS WHAT? He was NOT studying. He was watching RC racing videos on YouTube. Sooo... Sherry recorded it on her cell phone and walks back inside to ask Royce again what he was up to. And of course, he stuck to the lie, "I'm studying mom". Then she BUSTED him with the evidence by showing him the video footage.
What were you thinking buddy? You know your mom is smarter than that.

Sentiments from Cousin Jeffrey

  • Respectfully added by Shawn W. Feltmate for Cousin Jeffrey
  • 18 March 2024

1] I've spent time talking to Jeffrey about Royce. Jeffrey explained he was profoundly affected by Royce. Royce was more than a cousin, he was a friend, and a role model. He knew Royce as a gentle soul. He remembers together they would fish Aahme's pond, and have a great time. They went swimming one time and when they got out they found leeches on their bodies. Jeffrey had them around his nether region.

2] There was a time they met at Myrtle Beach for a family get-together one summer. They both ran around knocking on other guest's doors, then ducking and hiding (Ding Dong Ditch), and getting a real kick out of it. They hung out in the evenings meeting girls by the pool. It still makes Jeffrey laugh when he thinks of the good times they had. Royce was a true friend to Jeffrey.

Another Beautiful Thought

  • Uncle Shawn
  • 18 March 2024

One of the reasons Royce was so special to me, is because he wanted to know me. He wanted to know his Uncle Shawn. He took the time to ask questions. He asked me about the things I did and where I had been. It was a genuine quality he possessed. He was interested in all his family this way. His family was at a physical distance from him, but when he had time with them he made the most of it. He got to know them and understand who they are.
